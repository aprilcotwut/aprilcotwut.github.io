Hojicha Cajeta Kinako Cake

sooo this is actually 3 recipes but like

ingredients

for cake batter

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 extra egg yolk
  • 400g sugar
    • 500g flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 25g hojicha powder
  • 1 1/2 cup EVOO
  • 1 tsp vanilla

for dulce de leche

  • 1 cup goat milk
  • 1.5 cup whole milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 5g hojicha powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt

for diplomat creme

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 65g granulated sugar
  • 20g cornstarch
  • 1/8 tsp xantham gum
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 320 ml whole milk
  • 1.5 tbsp vanilla bean paste
  • 240 ml cream
  • 1/2 tsp lambda carrageenan
  • 1/8 tsp kappa carrageenan
  • 40g powdered sugar

for ermine frosting

  • 90g flour
  • 400g sugar
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 450g milk
  • 200g cream
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups (675g) butter
  • 75g kinako powder

directions

preheat oven 350 F

the xantham should be mixed w the sugar then blended in a blender, the lambda and kappa bloomed in boiling water then heated a lil longer w some of the cream

enjoy!!
