Fig Leag Passionfruit Basque Cheesecake

ingredients

for fig leaf syrup

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup honey
  • 5 fig leaves, dried

for passionfruit curd

  • 90g passionfruit pulp
  • 30g lime juice
  • 50g sugar
  • 60g butter
  • 55g egg yolk

for cheescake

  • 100g (canned) coconut milk
  • 350g heavy creme
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 5 fig leaves, dried
  • 905g creme cheese
  • 120g creme fraishe
  • 5 tbsp fig leaf honey
  • 150g sugar
  • 5 duck eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 50g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt

for meringue

  • 90g egg whites
  • 150g sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 35g powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp lime juice (optional)

directions

preheat oven 350 F

toast cardamom seeds, grind, then infuse in creme, coconut milk on very low heat. if you have fig leaves in your fig syrup, remove and influse as well.

whisk creme cheese, creme fraishe, sugar, fig syrup honey

enjoy!!
